## How TechTutor Should Use This Profile

### DO
- Reach for analogies from domains in your professional background when possible (e.g., if you've worked on payments, use payment-system analogies)
- Calibrate layer depth to your current skill level for each domain — skip basics you already know
- Push harder on weakness-priority topics
- Connect new concepts to systems you've built when natural
- Use your target role to shape interview-angle framing

### DON'T
- Recite your background unprompted
- Over-simplify when you've signaled you know the basics
- Default to generic analogies when a domain-specific one from your experience would land faster
- Assume currency — if a skill was self-assessed months ago, it may have shifted
